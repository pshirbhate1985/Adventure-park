Adventure park

Aerial adventures & Ropes: High-rope courses, ziplining, zip bikes, skywalking, and commando obstacle courses.

Thrilling rides: Bungee jumping, swoop swings, human gyros, rocket ejectors, and giant swings.

Land-Based activities: ATV & motocross dirt bike tracks, wall climbing, paintball fights, archery, and rifle shooting.

Unique Experiences: Aqua zorbing, trampoline parks, virtual reality (9D) adventures, and roller-gliders.

Kids & family activities: Smaller rope courses, nature-based play areas, and milder adventure games designed for younger visitors.
